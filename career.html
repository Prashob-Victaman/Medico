MEDICO-PASTORAL ASSOCIATION A Centre for Mental Health Care We are a 50 year old NGO, providing half way home rehabilitation facilities, both residential and day-care for persons with mental health issues. We have currently 35residents and 6 day care residents. Residents are under the treatment of their individual Psychiatrists.

Salary wil be fixed commensurate to qualification and experience. For more details of Medico Pastoral Association, please visit www.medicopastoralassociation.com
Interested candidates may apply giving their personal particulars, experience, salary currently drawn and salary expected. PF and ESI benefits available. Application may be sent by email to medicopastoralassociation@gmail.com with copies of supporting documents or by post to Medico Pastoral Association, #47, Pottery Road, Frazer Town, Bangalore-560005 to reach at the earliest and latest by 06th Jan 2025.
